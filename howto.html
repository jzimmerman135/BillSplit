1 / 7
Welcome to Billsplit. To get started, click on the "Get Started" link on the homepage or the "Split a Receipt" link on the header of any of the pages on the website.
2 / 7
Begin by entering the name of the first person to split with. To add more than one user, click the light blue arrow. When finished entering names, click on "Finish" to proceed to the next stage.
3 / 7
After entering all users, proceed by entering the name of the first purchased item to be split then click on the blue arrow to proceed.
4 / 7
After entering the name of the first item, the prompt will request for the price of the item. Enter the price of the item then click on the blue arrow to proceed.
5 / 7
Next, the prompt will request for the users with whom the item is to be shared amongst. To add users, add their initial to the textbox. For multiple users, enter their initials. For example, using the current image in the slideshow, one would enter [jt] to add John and Tom, or enter [all] to add all the members to the payment. Use the arrows to the right of the "Tax" text to modify the tax rate to be calculated for the product.
6 / 7
Next, enter another item and proceed to enter the amount and persons to split with in the following pages. If no more products need to be added, click the "Finish" button to perform the final calculation.
7 / 7
The breakdown calculation of the items entered will be calculated and displayed to you.
